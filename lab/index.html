Prompt Engineering Playground

Type a prompt and see an estimated token count, API cost, and a clarity score computed from the actual text.

Behind the viz: Tokens are estimated with the common ~4-characters-per-token heuristic blended with a word count. Cost uses published GPT-4o input pricing ($2.50 / 1M tokens). The clarity score is a transparent rubric that rewards specifics (numbers, an explicit format, a named audience) and penalizes vague filler and run-on sentences.

Seattle Daylight vs. Mood

How daylight hours track with seasonal mood across the year in Seattle.

Behind the viz: Monthly daylight hours for Seattle (47.6°N) plotted against a normalized seasonal mood index. The two move together — daylight, not rainfall, is the stronger signal for seasonal mood.

Meeting Load Through the Week

Meeting density by day and hour for a typical Principal DS manager.

Behind the viz: A day-by-hour heatmap of meeting density. Mid-week afternoons run hottest — which answers “why do I feel drained on Thursdays?”

PGA Tour Putting: Make % by Distance

How quickly a made putt becomes a coin flip as distance grows.

Behind the viz: Tour make-percentage by putt distance, drawn in a minimal, Tufte-inspired style. The 50/50 line lands right around eight feet.
