About

I'm a data science leader who helps organizations turn complexity into clear, confident decisions. As a Principal Data Science Manager at Microsoft, I build and lead teams that connect product data to strategy, growth, and risk.

I've led data science across Security and Productivity — two of Microsoft's largest product areas — partnering with product and executive leaders to set direction. My teams have turned trillions of product signals into insights that reduce risk, drive revenue, and give leadership the confidence to act.

My background in Information Science and Behavioral Psychology from Penn State shaped how I work: data problems are as much about people and systems as they are about technology.
