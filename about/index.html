About Me

I'm a data science leader who helps organizations turn complexity into clear, actionable decisions. I focus on metric development, product growth, and leading teams that turn data into business outcomes.

With a background in Information Science and Behavioral Psychology from Penn State, I understand that data problems are as much about people and systems as they are about technology.

I've led data science teams focused on Security and Productivity at Microsoft, partnering with product leaders to define strategy and growth. My work has turned trillions of product signals into insights that reduce risk, drive revenue, and enable confident executive decisions.

I believe in building simple solutions, earning trust, and helping teams focus on what matters. I'm not one of the new "AI experts" but I'm committed to understanding and applying it where it creates real value.
